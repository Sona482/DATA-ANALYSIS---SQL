High-Value Questions (10) for SQL-Driven E-Commerce Analysis

Here’s a refined list of 10 questions that are industry-relevant, actionable, and demonstrate SQL skills:

1.Top-selling products: Which products generate the highest revenue and quantity sold?

2.Customer contribution: Who are the top customers by revenue and order frequency?

3.Sales trends: How is revenue changing monthly/weekly over time?

4.Payment performance: How many payments succeeded vs failed, and are there delays?

5.Category performance: Which product categories generate the most revenue?

6.Repeat vs new customers: What proportion of sales comes from repeat customers?

7.High-value customers: Identify top 10% of customers contributing to overall revenue.

8.Geographical insights: Which cities/regions generate the most revenue?

9.Discount impact: How much revenue is generated from discounted products vs full price?

10.Order fulfillment efficiency: Average time from order placement to payment confirmation, and delayed orders.
